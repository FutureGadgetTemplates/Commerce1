Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Items must include all tags and accessories
  • Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned

How to Return

  1. Contact Us - Email us at {{ site.data.site.email }} with your order number and reason for return
  2. Receive Authorization - We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number and instructions
  3. Ship Your Return - Pack your item securely and ship it to our returns center
  4. Refund Processed - Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We're happy to help with exchanges. Follow the return process above and note that you'd like an exchange instead of a refund.

Refunds

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was defective or we made an error

Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund including shipping costs.
